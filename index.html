What is Frax Finance?

Frax Finance is adecentralized algorithmic stablecoin protocol that was launched in December2020. Founded by Sam Kazemian and Travis Moore, this innovative project seeksto address the inherent flaws of traditional fiat-pegged stablecoins like USDTand USDC.
Frax Finance aims to create astablecoin, FRAX, that is truly decentralized and maintains its stabilitythrough algorithmic mechanisms rather than relying on traditional collateralreserves. Unlike other stablecoins that are fully backed by fiat currency, Fraxuses a combination of collateral assets and algorithmic adjustments to keep itsvalue close to $1.

Collateral: FRAX is partially backed by collateralassets, mainly USDC, and other crypto assets.
